WHAT IS WILD RIFT?

Dive into Wild Rift: the skills-and-strats 5v5 MOBA experience of League of Legends, now built from the ground up for mobile and console. With fresh controls and fast-paced games, players of every level can team up with friends, lock in their champion, and go for the big plays.

Objective Of The Game

  1. Choose your Lane
  2. Take on the Jungle
  3. Destroy turret to reach the Nexus
  4. Destroy the enemy base

Champions

With over 40 champions to collect and play, you can find your favorites and show off your style.

Each champion has unique abilities, including a powerful ultimate ability, that you can unlock over the course of the game. Take Ahri, for example. She uses her raw magic to charm enemies one moment and obliterate them the next.
